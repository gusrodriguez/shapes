    Usage

    Click on the canvas and a red dot will be shown.

    Repeat this step three times and a paralellogram and a circle with the same area will be shown.

    Move the mouse over any red dot, and when it's highlighted you can drag it and resize the parallelogram altogether with the circle.

    You can reset the panel at any moment by clicking the Reset Panel button.
